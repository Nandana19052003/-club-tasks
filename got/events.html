Game of Thrones Chronological List of Major Events

Season 1

  • 1 AC: Aegon Targaryen begins his conquest of Westeros.
  • 298 AC: Jon Arryn dies under suspicious circumstances, leading to the beginning of the War of the Five Kings.
  • 298 AC: Eddard Stark is named Hand of the King by King Robert Baratheon.
  • 298 AC: Bran Stark is pushed from a tower and becomes paralyzed.
  • 298 AC: Daenerys Targaryen marries Khal Drogo and becomes pregnant.

Season 2

  • 298 AC: Stannis Baratheon declares himself the rightful king and begins his campaign for the Iron Throne.
  • 298 AC: Theon Greyjoy takes Winterfell and claims it for the Iron Islands.
  • 298 AC: Renly Baratheon is assassinated by Melisandre's shadow baby.
  • 298 AC: Daenerys Targaryen's dragons are stolen by Pyat Pree in Qarth.
  • 299 AC: Battle of Blackwater: Stannis Baratheon's army is defeated by Tywin Lannister and the Tyrells.
